    • About me

    I am an Associate Professor at the Division of Computational Biology, Mayo Clinic. I got my Ph.D. in Genomics and Computational Biology with emphasis in Biostatistics and M.S. in statistics from the University of Pennsylvania. Currently my research focuses on the development and application of powerful and robust statistical methods for high-dimensional omics data, especially for microbiome and single-cell sequencing data.
